The game is called "Save Master Piggy" and is played by two players. On each turn, the current player rolls the rock, which moves up and down above Master Piggy. The challenge is to avoid hitting Master Piggy with the rock. If the player successfully avoids hitting Master Piggy, their score increases by 1 point. If the player hits Master Piggy, their score is reset to zero, and their turn ends.

The first player to reach a score of 30 points or higher wins. However, players must be careful not to hit Master Piggy, or their score will be reset to zero.
